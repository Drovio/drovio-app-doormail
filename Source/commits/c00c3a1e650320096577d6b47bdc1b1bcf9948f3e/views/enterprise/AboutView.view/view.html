doorMail - Follow-up emails and customer engagement

Through this application you can engage with your users quick and easy. Supported features:

  • • Create and send emails to your users
  • • Create and manage lists
  • • Create email templates
  • • Action history, record every email sent

NOTE: This isn't a newsletter application, so the content of the emails must be personalized without including ads or other type of campaigns.
